About Me

My name is Oluwadolapo "Dolapo" Atobiloye. I am a second-year Bachelor's of Science student studying Computer Science at Rochester Insitute of Technology with a minor in Game Design & Development and an Immersion in Theater Arts.

I have experience as a Software Engineering intern in Terragon Group located in Lagos Nigeria. I have also completed multiple computer science and web design courses in my time at RIT.

I am a huge fan of musical theater and while I'm not much of a singer, I consider myself a budding actress. I originated the role of Yashira in the origianl play AI-Pollo by David Munnell. I also performed as #8 in RIT performing arts scholarship's production of "The Wolves". I try to abide by the motto "Live while you can", which basically means try everything and is how I got into musicals in the first place.
